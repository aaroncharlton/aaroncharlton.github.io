Research

My research is focused on understanding and improving the practice of scientific research within the field of marketing. I created OpenMKT.org, a website aimed at increasing transparency and quality in marketing science.

Teaching

I have taught marketing research, digital marketing and marketing communications at University of Oregon and Illinois State University. Students in my classes have worked with real-world clients in order to gain hands-on experience. There was no book for this sort of thing so I wrote the book (and it's free): Real World Clients: A Guide for Student Consultants.

Consulting

I also have quite a bit of experience in real-world marketing, with an emphasis on digital marketing. I optimize websites for conversion and search engine ranking and I drive traffic to websites through both paid (ads) and organic methods.
