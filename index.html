Aaron Charlton, PhD, MBA
Marketing Researcher

My research is focused on understanding and improving the practice of scientific research within the field of marketing. I created OpenMKT.org, a website aimed at increasing transparency and quality in marketing science.

I have taught marketing research and marketing communications at University of Oregon and Illinois State University. Students in my classes have worked with real-world clients in order to gain hands-on experience. There was no book for this sort of thing so I wrote the book (and it's free): Real World Clients: A Guide for Student Consultants.
